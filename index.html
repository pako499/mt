Our Company

Maruti Textile —
Woven with Purpose

Maruti Textile is a textile business built on the fundamentals that actually matter — consistent quality, reliable delivery, and relationships that last. Co-founded by Shlok and Priya Nemani, the company has grown steadily by doing things the right way, not just the fast way.

We operate end-to-end across the textile supply chain. That means we are involved at every stage — from procurement and production planning to quality checks, logistics coordination, and client servicing. Nothing is outsourced in terms of responsibility. When something needs to get done, our team gets it done.

  • ✦ 50–60 professionals across operations, sales, finance & logistics
  • ✦ End-to-end textile operations under one roof
  • ✦ Strong vendor & client relationships built over years
  • ✦ Quality checks at every stage of production
  • ✦ Lean, agile business that moves when the market moves

Our People

The Team Behind
Everything We Build

A business is only as strong as the people who show up every day. At Maruti Textile, that strength is real — and it comes from a team that takes its work seriously.

🏗️

Operations & Production

Our operations team keeps the business moving without gaps. They coordinate production timelines, manage procurement, handle logistics, and make sure what leaves our facility meets the standard we've set — every single time. It's unglamorous work, and they do it exceptionally well.

🤝

Sales & Client Relations

Our sales team doesn't just close deals — they build partnerships. They understand our clients' businesses, anticipate needs, and communicate clearly at every stage. The repeat business we earn is a direct reflection of how this team shows up for people, not just transactions.

📋

Finance & Accounts

Behind every healthy business is a finance team that keeps it honest. Ours handles everything from daily accounts and vendor payments to financial planning and compliance — with the discipline and accuracy that gives the entire organisation confidence in where we stand.

🔍

Quality & Compliance

Quality is not a department — it's a mindset. But we have people who make it their specific job to ensure our products leave in perfect condition. They catch what others might miss, ask the uncomfortable questions, and hold the line on standards when pressure builds to cut corners.

What It's Like to Work Here

We're not a company that runs on slogans. We're a mid-sized business that works hard, communicates directly, and tries to treat every person — employee, vendor, or client — with basic respect and fairness. People who join Maruti Textile tend to stay, and that says more about the culture than any words on a website.

Growth is real here. When someone shows initiative, they get more responsibility. When someone puts in the work, it gets noticed. We're not perfect, but we're a team that's genuinely trying to build something worth being part of.
